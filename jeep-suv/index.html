As far as car collections go, the Jeep SUV range has to be the most diverse. From the compact competency of the Jeep Renegade to the outstanding performance of the Jeep Grand Cherokee Trackhawk, the seven-slot grille is worn by a superb selection of five of the most capable SUVs you’ll find on the road.

Built for everyday activities, as well as more intrepid expeditions, you’ll find a Jeep SUV for every need. Each model is very different, with all boasting the excellent handling that comes with a choice of sophisticated 4x4 systems. Join us as we discover the Jeep SUV range, and head off for adventure...

Jeep Renegade Adventure Awaits

The strong Jeep Renegade is an everyday compact 4x4, carefully crafted to be equally at home in the supermarket car park as it is off-road. Wherever you go, you can be sure that this Jeep SUV will stand out with distinctive styling and a huge range of customisable exterior features, such as My Sky open air roof system, roof/door decals and roof bars, as well as a host of interior options, like rear view camera, cargo nets and premium mats

Four different trim levels provide cloth or leather upholstery, LED lamps and second row USB ports**.

The excellent safety features of the Jeep Renegade are constant throughout the range, including front and rear side-curtain airbags, a driver’s knee airbag and front seat-mounted side airbags. A reassuring drive to any destination.

Jeep Compass Find Your Way

With an SUV like the Jeep Compass under your command, you’ll have no difficulty finding your road… and sticking to it! An updated aerodynamic profile distinguishes it from more blocky compact SUVs on the market while still being impressively good-looking and spacious.

The latest technology equips this Jeep SUV with 70 safety features, including six airbags and provides a sympathetically designed, driver-centred cabin. A 7-inch, colour instrument panel** and superb connectivity allow you to concentrate on your driving, distraction-free.

A range of fuel-efficient petrol and diesel powertrain options and 4x4 systems provide up to a claimed 64.2 mpg. While you don’t have to climb a mountain to enjoy this Jeep SUV, it’ll make you feel like you’re on top of the world!

Jeep Cherokee Stylishly Brave

The Jeep Cherokee proves that you don’t have to break a sweat when in search of excitement. The premium finish of this mid-sized Jeep SUV shouts ‘luxury’ at every turn so whatever your day has in store, you’ll arrive relaxed, refreshed and ready for action.

More robust than the Compass or Renegade, this Jeep SUV boasts a five star EURO NCAP safety rating thanks to technology like Adaptive Cruise Control Plus which monitors the distance to the vehicle in front, as well as LaneSense™ and Lane Departure Warning Plus system**.

Practicality comes in bucket loads with a wading depth of up to 508mm (3.2L option) and several 9-speed automatic transmission choices. Make use of the optional Technology Group, Cold Weather Group, Comfort & Convenience Group or Safety & Security Group packs to tailor your Jeep Cherokee SUV to your every motoring need.

Jeep Wrangler Ready for Anything

The iconic Jeep Wrangler is the perfect companion for intrepid driving, whether you choose the 2-door or 4-door option. 32” tyres offer water fording capability of up to 760 mm for additional practicality while removable doors and roof allow you to experience the real outdoors.

It’s been around for at least 30 years, but you can be sure that the latest Jeep Wrangler models possess the highest quality materials and most up-to-date technology including an Alpine® Premium Audio System for a safe, convenient and comfortable drive.

A choice of automatic petrol or diesel engines and Stop/Start as standard ensure the best possible efficiency. With Park Assist, Blind Spot Monitoring and Rear Cross Path Detection, every drive in the Jeep Wrangler doesn’t have to be an adventure… unless you want it to be!

Jeep Grand Cherokee Unbeatable

With the Jeep Grand Cherokee, anything is possible! And we can’t wait for the newest release expected later this year. A supercharged 6.2-litre V8 petrol engine option and five Selec-Trac™ dynamic driving modes will make it the ultimate performance Jeep SUV. The less daring amongst us might prefer the more sedate 3.0-litre V6 diesel, but with so many luxurious interior features, there’s no need to sacrifice comfort for practicality on either choice.

Performance will be monitored by real-time readouts via the Uconnect touchscreen, giving you information at your fingertips, whenever and wherever you need it.

Leather sports seats, sat nav, and Apple/Android connectivity will allow you to make the most of every drive while blind spot monitoring, rear cross-traffic assist and park assist makes every manoeuvre a breeze.

A sculpted bonnet and colour-coded wheel flares ensure that you look fantastic as you drive away in what’s expected to be one of the most capable Grand Cherokees yet.
