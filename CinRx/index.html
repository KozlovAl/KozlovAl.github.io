CinRx Pharmaâ€™s primary objective is to create drug-specific and thesis-specific companies that will be attractive acquisition targets to large pharma and biotech companies. CinRx Pharma establishes and manages these entities based on technology that is sourced in house, acquired, partnered, and/or in-licensed from partners. Target companies will be capitalized by CinVestment Capital, the investment vehicle managed by CinRx Pharma. As companies are formed and capitalized, CinRx Pharma works with Medpace, a global CRO, to establish and execute a clinical development plan.

Companies are run virtually and with a distributed management strategy, in which CinRx Pharma staff and external consultants can be actively involved with multiple companies at any given time. This allows for capital efficiency at the company level. We look for assets or companies that are pre-clinical to Phase II. Our aim is to capitalize these companies (with or without partners) through Phase II or an appropriate value inflection point.
